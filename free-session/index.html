WHAT'S THE CATCH

Why Is It Free?

There's no catch.  We've found most parents prefer a free training to feel things out.  See if we know what we're doing, and if we can actually help your Athlete level up.

And we want to know if you're a good fit for us too! It doesn't help us or you if you're in the wrong program.  A 30 minute session is the simplest way to make sure we get your player the support they need.  If we believe we can help, we'll make you an offer for a specific training program.  Either way you get 30 minutes of Training.

Who this is for

Experienced Players Only

This is for players with two plus years of competitive experience, goals of playing Varsity, College and Pro, who are looking for more individualized training to take their games to the next level.

If your athlete is new to the game, only played Jazz, starting their first year of Bantam, we'd recommend the come to Group Training.  If they're young, Kindergarten-2nd grade and looking to get started, we'd recommend our Rookie Group Class.

HOW CAN TRAINING ZONE HELP

Our Training Programs

Our training programs are designed for elite players, or players who've shown the drive and dedication to become elite.  These are highly individual programs, tailored to specific needs.  Our 90 Day Shot Program for elite shooters, 90 Day Player Dev for scorers and play makers, and our Academy for players who want to double their time in the gym.  The goal of the Free Training, is to figure out your players needs, and which training programs can help them most.
