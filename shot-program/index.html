How it works

Three phases. One shot.

A shot isn't created overnight. It's built one step at a time.

PHASE 1

Discover

Weeks 1–4

We film your shot, break it down frame by frame, and identify the areas you can improve. Then we build the mechanics — base, hand placement, release — with hands-on reps until the new motion holds.

PHASE 2

Build

Weeks 5–8

Rep your new shot. Study film of your shot, custom workouts, and rep until the adjustments to your form consistently hold under game-speed reps.

PHASE 3

Range

Weeks 9–12

Game speed, game range. Off the catch, off the move, off the dribble — plus game film breakdown, so the new shot shows up where it counts: in games.

    FAQ

    Frequently asked.

    Who is this for?

    Athletes in 7th grade and older who are serious about changing their shot. Younger players: we run Jr Shot Camps and individual sessions — ask about those on the call.

    How is this different from Shot Camp?

    Shot Camp is a 3-day introduction to how we think about shooting. The Shot Program is the full transformation — 90 days, one-on-one coached sessions, video breakdown, and a tracked, staged progression. Camp shows you the system; the Program rebuilds your shot with it.

    What if my athlete plays other sports or has team practice?

    We build the schedule around your athlete's season — team practice and games come first, and the sessions fit around them. We'll map it out together on the call.

    How do we know it's working?

    Every session is tracked in our training app. You'll see the workouts assigned, the sessions completed, and the numbers moving — plus video of the shot at the start and as it changes. Progress here is measured, not promised.

    What happens after the 90 days?

    Graduates can continue on the Maintenance Plan ($497/mo, optional): custom workouts, tracked metrics, and continued access to the system. Most athletes keep going — a rebuilt shot stays sharp with structured reps.

    Why do I have to book a call to sign up?

    Ten spots means fit matters more than speed. The call is where we learn about your athlete's shot and goals — and where you get to ask us anything before committing to 90 days.
